What is FitnessKey and how it works?

FitnessKey is an online platform where you can buy daily pass/es for any fitnessclub/gym as per your choice.
Daily Pass- Pay per Use- No Contracts.

Using FitnessKey is very simple as mentioned in "How it works" section. The steps are as follows:

  • 1. Find and select any FitnessClub/Gym near you.
  • 2. Buy daily pass online for the selected Club/gym.
  • 3. Show your pass at Club/gym and enter.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on "Sign Up" tab on the top right corner of any page.
For mobile version, "Sign Up" tab could be found inside sandwich on the left.
Sign Up can be done using Facebook or Google Account or your Email.
Note: Creation of account is absolutely free!.

How do I buy a Pass?

As mentioned in "How it works": To buy a pass, make a selection of FitnessClub/Gym and buy available pass/passes of your choice by clicking on "Buy Pass" to Purchase.
When you purchase a Pass, you will receive a SMS and Email with Pass Code.
Show that Pass Code at FitnessClub/Gym for entry.

Pass Validity

Pass will be valid for 15 days from the Date/Time of purchase. You can use the pass only once during the valid time period. Once you use the pass to enter the Fitness Center, it will expire automatically.

When can I access the FitnessClub/Gym after pass purchase?

Immediately! When you purchase a gym pass online, a pass code is sent to you immediately by e-mail and SMS. You simply show this pass code at the Fitness Center/Gym for entry.

Can I become a member of a FitnessClub/Gym using FitnessKey?

If you are interested in becoming a member of a FitnessClub/Gym that you see on our site, please contact them directly.

How do I find the list of FitnessClub/Gym near to me OR location mentioned by me?

On Mobile device: It will automatically show the list and the map version of the FitnessClub/Gym nearby area using GPS. Location can be changed by manually entering it.
On Desktop: Write your location and click on "Search Pass", it will show all the FitnessClub/Gym in that location and nearby.
Also, map could be used to pinpoint your location and find the FitnessClub/Gym near you.

How do I buy a FitnessClub/Gym pass?

Once you have selected a FitnessClub/Gym of your choice, you will be able to see the pass options available for that facility.
To buy the pass, click on "Buy Now" (if not logged in, you will be prompted to sign up or login).
On successful purchase of pass, you will receive a SMS and Email with pass details.

Can I transfer my pass to someone else?

No, the pass is non-transferable.

I can't see FitnessClub/Gym of my choice?

If you have any specific Club/Gym in your mind, which is not listed in our site, please let us know.
We will talk to club/gym owner and try to make them the part of FitnessKey.

What cards do you accept?

We accept Visa, MasterCard and American Express.

Can I pay at the gym?

No, you cannot pay at FitnessClub/Gym directly, you need to purchase pass online..
Also, to avail FitnessKey offers you should visit our website and purchase from there.

I didn't receive my gym pass by SMS/e-mail.

Normally, it takes few minutes to receive an SMS and Email with your Pass details. In case, you have not received it please contact our support (Email ID or Phone Number)

How to cancel pass?

To cancel an unused pass, please contact our Support (Email/Phone number).
A pass can be cancelled within 14 days of date/time of purchase. Also, a cancellation fee of 10% is applicable on each cancelation.

How do you decide the gym pass prices?

Rights of decision for Pass Price lie with FitnessClub/Gym. Our team discuss with them to agree upon a best discounted price which we offer to our clients.
You can also give your suggestions based on the market which could be used to fetch best prices for our clients.

I have deleted the SMS/E-mail with my pass code.

Not an issue. If you have accidently deleted the SMS or Email containing your Pass code, you can anytime retrieve them by clicking on purchases on our website and resend them to you SMS or Email.

When is my gym pass valid from?

Gym passes and bundles are valid from date of purchase. You can start using them immediately.

How old do I have to be to buy a gym pass?

18 years is the minimum age to buy online pass from FitnessKey.
Note: Each FitnessClub/Gym could have separate age requirements.

Can I use my gym pass at different gyms?

No. Your gym pass is only valid at the FitnessClub/Gym it was purchased for.

Do you share my contact details with the FitnessClub/Gym?

No. We will not share your contact details with FitnessClub/Gym OR anyone else.
